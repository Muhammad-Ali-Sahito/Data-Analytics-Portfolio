## Files

- hr Employees Dashboard.pbix

## Project Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to analyze employee attrition and workforce trends. It helps HR managers understand why employees leave the company and identify key factors affecting employee retention.

## Key Insights

- Analyzed employee attrition across different departments.
- Compared attrition by gender and age groups.
- Analyzed attrition by job role and marital status.
- Created an interactive dashboard using Power BI.
