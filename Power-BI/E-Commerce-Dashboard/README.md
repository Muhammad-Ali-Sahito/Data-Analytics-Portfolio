# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes e-commerce sales performance and provides insights into sales, customers, products, payment methods, and profit margins. It helps identify sales trends, customer behavior, and business performance using interactive visualizations.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Sales
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Highest Sales
- Average Sales

### Visualizations
- Sales by Product Category
- Sales by Payment Method
- Quantity Sold by Product
- Orders by Order Status
- Sales by Customer Location (Map)
- Profit Margin by Product Category

### Interactive Slicers
- Date
- Product Category
- Payment Method
